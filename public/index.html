My Process

The approach I take to tackling a project relies heavily on my process. As a programmer, it’s important to me that there is a sequence of events I take to achieve a desired outcome. It allows me to improve aspects of a project systematically.

Imagine

One of my favorite tools to use when molding an idea for an application is Keynote. I like to create slides of a mock user interface. Sometimes, the Keynote slides are a major source of inspiration for the design phase. Being fascinated by design, I also like to think of logos in this step.

Brainstorm

Wether I’m working on a project with a team or by myself, I like to create a user story and entity relation table. This is step is solely back-end related. It will enable me to understand the functionality required to create a the part of the app that will control the storage, acquisition, and security of data.

Design

A more sophisticated and beautiful tool that I use for the design phase is Adobe XD. This is my favorite step in the process. I love thinking about how a user will interact with my applications and how to carefully guide them along the way. I find inspiration in everything from ad’s to other sites and even poster designs.

Create

Breathing life into a design using a sequence of characters is the most exciting part if the process. I am well versed in the Ruby on Rails framework and really love it for creating API’s for my ReactJS front-ends to consume. I also am familiar with GitHub, Heroku, PostgresQL, Bootstrap, SQL, and Javascript.
